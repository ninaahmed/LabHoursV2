Hello, Welcome to 314 Lab Hours!

Here are some instructions on how to use this website.


Joining the Line

  1. Click the Join tab.
  2. Check if the queue is open. You won't be able to submit your information if it's closed.
  3. Enter in your information if it's open.
  4. Click Join the Queue!

You will be redirected to View, where your name should appear at the bottom of the page.

Please note: You don't have to enter your actual UT EID if you don't want to, but you should enter unique text, because you will be able to use this entry to remove yourself from the queue. We don't want others accidentally removing you from the queue.

If you try to add yourself to the queue when you're already in there, you'll lose your previous spot in line and will be moved to the end. You can add yourself again after you've been helped!

Please join the Zoom meeting once you start approaching the front of the line. The instructors will have a breakout room to help students in, so feel free to wait in the main room.

Also, expect an email in your inbox telling you your place in line and what you entered in the EID field.


Viewing the Line

This one is pretty easy, just click the View tab.

If you're on a tight schedule, we recommend checking this tab out before joining the line.


Removing Yourself From the Line

  1. Click the Remove Self tab.
  2. Enter in your UT EID, or whatever you entered in that field. If you don't remember what you entered, check your email.
  3. Click submit.

Joining the Zoom Meeting

This one is also simple. You can click on the Current Zoom Meeting tag, and it will open a new tab that redirects you to the zoom meeting.

Please note: The most reliable way to join the current Zoom meeting is through the links on the Calendar on Canvas. The instructors change the Zoom meeting link on the website themselves whenever a new block starts. So if the link doesn't work one day, go to Canvas.


If you are having any issues with the website, please email 314labhours@gmail.com or make a Piazza post.
